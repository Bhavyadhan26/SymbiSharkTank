What We Offer

Symbi Shark Tank is a dynamic platform dedicated to fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and collaboration among budding startups and seasoned investors.

At Symbi Shark Tank, we believe in the power of symbiotic relationships, where entrepreneurs find the support they need to thrive, and investors discover promising opportunities to fuel their portfolios. We serve as a nexus where bright minds converge, ideas flourish, and partnerships flourish.

Our platform offers a unique blend of resources, including pitch events, networking opportunities, mentorship programs, and access to capital, all geared towards empowering entrepreneurs to transform their visions into reality. Whether you're a startup seeking funding or an investor scouting for the next big thing, Symbi Shark Tank provides the ideal environment to connect, collaborate, and catalyze success. Join us in shaping the future of innovation. Together, let's dive into the depths of possibility and emerge victorious, creating a symbiotic ecosystem where ideas soar and dreams take flight.

Aakash Banda

• The co-founder and CMO of Boze, Aakash is one of the four sharks on SST. He is a CA (Chartered Accountant) by profession and then went on to finish his first MBA in Finance and Strategy from Indian School of Business and second MBA from Kellogg Graduate School of Management in the US.
• Boze today is one of the most sought-after headset equipment models in India and is known to have gained a market share of 27.3% market share.

Jiya Agarwal

• The CEO and co-founder of FLOW cosmetics, she is a Shark on SST. Born and raised in Delhi, she is an alumnus of IIT Madras and IIM Ahmedabad. Known for her entrepreneurial spirit, she turned down a lucrative INR 1 crore job offer from a leading investment bank to pursue her own business.
• As the CEO of FLOW cosmetics, she has led the brand to become one of India's fastest-growing beauty brands. With over 35,000 branded retail outlets across nearly 500 cities, she serves as an inspiration to many Indian women embarking on their own entrepreneurial journeys.

Saachi Shetty

• The executive director at Cure Pharma and one of India leading businesswoman, Shetty was born in Pune, Maharashtra and is a CA by profession. She went on to get her MBA from Duke University's Fuqua School of Business. She is one of the Shark on SST.
• She joined Cure as its CFO after working in the US for close to 6 years. She is also involved in multiple government programs including NITI Aayog's Women's Entrepreneurship Platform.

Bhavya Dhanuka

• Founder and CEO of Key Chain Access, with a net worth of close to $50 million, he is one of the four sharks on the show. He is an alum of Boston College and started his entrepreneurial journey post his master's - laying the groundwork for Key Chain Access.
• He also sits on the boards of Zepo and advises Wireshark, Grip and Kae Capital. Bhavya describes himself as an entrepreneur, investor (A prominent investor in SST), explorer and eternal optimist who has 200+ Investments in Tech & Brand Start-ups.
