Our Mission

We've just started this platform for the youth to come up as excellent entrepreneurs.

Symbi Shark Tank is a dynamic platform dedicated to fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and collaboration among budding startups and seasoned investors.

The mission of Symbi Shark Tank is to serve as a catalyst for entrepreneurial growth and innovation by providing a platform for aspiring Students to showcase their ideas, receive valuable feedback, and access resources necessary to turn their visions into reality.

We aim to foster a supportive ecosystem that nurtures creativity, fosters collaboration, and empowers individuals to pursue their entrepreneurial dreams.
